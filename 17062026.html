My Travel Bucket List

Traveling broadens the mind and enriches the soul. Below you'll find my top destinations ranked by priority, the must-do activities, and essential packing items for every trip.

Top Destinations (By Priority)

  1. Kyoto, Japan
  2. Santorini, Greece
  3. Machu Picchu, Peru
  4. Cape Town, South Africa
  5. Queenstown, New Zealand

Must-Do Activities

  1. Hike a famous trail
  2. Try authentic local cuisine
  3. Visit a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  4. Attend a local festival or event
  5. Learn a few phrases of the local language

Packing Essentials

  • Passport and travel documents
  • Universal power adapter
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Reusable water bottle
  • First-aid kit

Travel Tips

  • Book flights at least 2 months in advance
  • Always keep a digital copy of your documents
  • Notify your bank before traveling abroad
  • Purchase travel insurance
  • Research local customs and etiquette
